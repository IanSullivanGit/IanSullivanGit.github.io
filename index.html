About Me

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics at UC Davis. I am co-advised by Eugene Gorsky and Melissa Zhang.

I study low-dimensional topology and link homology theories. More specifically, I am interested in manifolds up to dimension 5, quantum/Floer theoretic link homology theories, categorification, and how these topics coalesce.
